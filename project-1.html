Digital Record Room

2024

I developed a digital record room system for the Moratuwa Divisional Secretariat Office. The goal was to make it easier to locate physical records stored in their manual racks, almost like a library management system. Staff could log in with authentication, and depending on their role (admin, staff, or viewer) they had different levels of access. The backend was built entirely with Flask, and I exposed a REST API so that the system could be extended later or connected to other services. This was my first ever backend project, before this I had only worked with Tkinter. I learned Flask specifically for this build, in a short time, and it taught me how to think about authentication, role-based permissions, and structuring APIs in a real-world context.
